Section 1 — heading "IF YOU EXPERIENCE":

- Frequent stomach aches
- Loose stools or undigested food in stool
- Low energy compared to peers
- Catches colds easily
- Sweats easily during sleep

8. Serve warm in small portions

Section 4 — heading "WHEN TO EAT/DRINK IT":

- Replace one regular meal per day for 1 week
- Small portions (half a bowl) are better than large ones
- Serve warm, never cold

Section 5 — heading "HOW LONG UNTIL YOU FEEL BETTER":

- After 1 week: more formed stools
- After 2-3 weeks: steadier energy, fewer colds
- Continue 2-3 times per week as the child grows
