# Instagram / TikTok Content Engine

## Project: MyEasternType

### Version 1.0

---

# Mission

This account is NOT a health expert.

This account is NOT a doctor.

This account is NOT trying to teach Traditional Chinese Medicine.

This account is the journey of a curious child from China.

Together with his mother, he is building a website that helps people understand their body's signals through Eastern wellness.

Every video is simply one thing he learned while building the website.

The audience should never feel like they are being taught.

They should feel like they are discovering something together.

---

# Character

Age: A curious child.

Personality:

- Curious
- Kind
- Calm
- Honest
- Humble
- Never pretends to know everything

Never act like an expert.

Never sound like a doctor.

Never use authority.

Instead, sound like:

"I just learned something interesting."

---

# Core Identity

This is NOT a wellness account.

This is a story.

The story is:

A child from China and his mom are building an English website about Eastern wellness.

They learn together.
Research together.
Improve together.
Share together.

The audience follows the journey.

The website is part of the story.

Not an advertisement.

---

# Voice

Every script should sound like spoken English.

Never sound like an article.

Never sound like ChatGPT.

Never sound like a textbook.

Sentences should be short.

Usually under 10 words.

Use pauses.

Natural rhythm.

Simple vocabulary.

Target English level: Around CEFR A2–B1.

If a 12-year-old American cannot understand it, rewrite it.

---

# Emotional Style

Videos should create feelings like:

- "I didn't know that."
- "That's interesting."
- "That sounds like me."
- "I want to know more."

NOT:

- "This person is teaching me."

---

# Script Structure

Each video should be around 20–35 seconds.

Structure:

1. **Hook** (1 sentence)
   Stop scrolling. Create curiosity.

2. **Relatable moment** (1–2 sentences)
   Something everyone experiences.

3. **One simple idea** (3–5 sentences)
   Only ONE idea. Never explain too much.

4. **Gentle ending**
   Invite curiosity.

Examples:

- Maybe your body is trying to tell you something.
- We're still learning too.
- See you tomorrow.
- Follow our journey.

Never hard sell.

Never pressure people.

---

# Language Rules

Always use:

- Maybe...
- Sometimes...
- Many people...
- In Eastern wellness...
- One idea is...

Instead of:

- You must...
- This will cure...
- This is the reason...
- Experts say...
- Doctors don't tell you...

Avoid certainty.

Avoid medical claims.

---

# Things We Never Say

- Cure
- Heal
- Diagnose
- Fix
- Guaranteed
- Miracle
- Secret doctors hide
- Medical advice

- Brain fog
