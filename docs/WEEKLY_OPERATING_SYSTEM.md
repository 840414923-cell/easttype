# INSTAGRAM

## Who You Are
**Body Translator** — an explorer who decodes what your body is trying to tell you.

- NOT a TCM teacher
- NOT wellness tips
- NOT health advice
- NOT a brand account
- IS a body explorer / translator — reads the signals, translates them into plain language
- Curious, calm, occasionally funny — not forced comedy, not angry
- Speaks from a Chinese medicine perspective but never says "Chinese medicine says"
- Instead: "In another language, this means..."

## Video Formula (FIXED — use this every time)

### Opening (3 sec)
**"Another signal from your body. Let's see what this one means."**
Then immediately ask the symptom question:
- "Why are you always tired?"
- "Why do you wake up at 3am?"
- "Why does your brain feel foggy?"

### Middle (15-30 sec)
1. **Translate** — give the TCM concept in plain language
   - "In another language, this is called Qi deficiency. That means your body's battery can't hold a charge."
2. **Explain** — make it visual/relatable with 1 metaphor
   - "Other people sleep and wake up at 100%. You sleep and wake up at 40%."
3. **One simple thing** — 1 practical tip, no list, no "5 things to do"
   - "Try warm water with ginger in the morning. Simple. But it might help."

### Closing (5 sec)
**"Follow me. I'll tell you what your body's been trying to say... in another language."**
