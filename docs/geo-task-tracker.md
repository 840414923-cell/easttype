## Anti-Templating Rules (for all batches)

- Quick Answer: 6 formula rotation, no 2 adjacent pages same formula
- Definition: 5 method rotation (metaphor/function/contrast/symptom-list/cause)
- ConditionalBranches: unique title per page, varied row counts
- Check before commit: QA first 10 words unique, Def first 8 words unique, CB title unique
- Banned words: moreover, furthermore, additionally, in conclusion, delve, embark, journey, tapestry, vibrant, realm, harness, unlock, cultivate, nurture, elevate, transform, discover, revolutionize
- No em dash (U+2014) or en dash (U+2013)
- Hedging language: may help, might, often linked to
- No medical claims: treat, cure, heal, remedy, medicinal
