# Brand Soul

## Project: MyEasternType

---

We are not building followers.

We are inviting people to grow with us.

---

# Who We Are

A child from China and his mom.

Building something together.

Learning out loud.

Sharing what they find.

Not experts.

Not teachers.

Just curious.

---

# What We Believe

Every body has a language.

Most people never learned to listen.

Eastern wellness is not mysterious.

It is practical.

It is human.

It is about paying attention.

---

# What We Will Never Do

We will never pretend to be doctors.

We will never promise cures.

We will never use fear to sell.

We will never say "you must."

We will never act like we know everything.

---

# What We Will Always Do

We will always say "maybe."

We will always say "in Eastern wellness."

We will always admit what we don't know.

We will always keep learning.

We will always be honest.

---

# Our Tone

Gentle.

Curious.

Humble.

Never loud.

Never urgent.

Never authoritative.

Like a friend sharing something interesting over tea.

Not a professor giving a lecture.

---

# Our Story

We started because we were curious.

Why do Chinese people drink warm water?

Why does my grandma make this tea?

Why does my body feel different in winter?

We asked questions.

We found answers.

We built a website.

We are still learning.

The story is not finished.

That is the point.

---

# How People Should Feel

When they find us:

"This is interesting."

When they watch a video:

"That sounds like me."

When they visit the website:

"I want to understand my body better."

When they leave:

"Maybe I'll come back tomorrow."

Never:

"I need to buy this."

"I have to sign up."

"This person is selling me something."

---

# The Website

The website is not a product.

It is part of the journey.

It is where the learning lives.

The quiz is not a funnel.

It is a mirror.

"Here is what your body might be telling you."

Not:

"Buy this to fix your problem."

---

# Growth

We grow slowly.

We grow by being useful.

We grow by being honest.

We grow by being consistent.

We do not grow by being loud.

We do not grow by being aggressive.

One person who truly trusts us

is worth more than

ten thousand followers who don't.

---

# Long Game

We are not here for this month.

We are here for years.

Every video. Every post. Every word.

They all add up.

They all build trust.

Or they don't.

That is why every word matters.

---

# Final Rule

If you are not sure whether something fits our brand,

ask this question:

"Would a curious child say this?"

If the answer is no, don't say it.
